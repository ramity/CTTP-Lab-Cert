Dear Drs. Hall and Williams,

Pine Instruments has developed a maintenance procedure that is recommended for all AFG2 Superpave Gyratory Compactors.  The AFG2 has three angle sensors that provide data to its computerized motion controller.  Protective covers minimize the possibility of sensor damage caused by material spillage during the compaction process.  However, the metallic portions of the sensors are not immune to chemical oxidation.  Built-up corrosion may inhibit movement of the sensor mechanism and erroneous data will be sent to the motion controller.  If this occurs, the AFG2 may display a numerical error code, and compaction of asphalt samples will not be possible.  Several customers have recently been affected by temporary angle sensor malfunctions.

The new maintenance procedure is applicable as preventive maintenance on fully operational instruments, as well as corrective action on those that have experienced a malfunction.  Pine recommends all AFG2 customers perform preventive maintenance as soon as practical.  In conjunction with the maintenance procedure, a control software upgrade incorporates improvements over earlier software revisions.  The software upgrade and all necessary instructions are available at the following links:

1. www.pineinst.com/hold/AFG2_Software_Upgrade.pdf

2. www.pineinst.com/hold/AFG2_V0907b.zip

3. www.pineinst.com/hold/AFG2_AngleSensor.pdf

The first link has instructions for upgrading your AFG2 control software to the latest revision.  The second link is a compressed file of the new control software components, Version 09.07b/668.  The third link has instructions for the maintenance procedure, which requires the new control software and a few inexpensive items readily available at most hardware stores.   Please read the instructions provided and decide if you feel comfortable performing the work yourself or prefer a service technician.  Finally, the latest AFG2 operation manual is also available here: 

http://www.pineinst.com/test/pdf/LMAFG2.pdf

You can contact me by phone or email if you have questions.  To ensure Pine has accurate information about your AFG2A S/N 8001, please notify me when the software upgrade and maintenance procedure are complete.
